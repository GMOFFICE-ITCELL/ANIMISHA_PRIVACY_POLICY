Privacy Policy — SCR Animisha APP

Effective Date: 02-12-2024  |  Last Updated: 10-09-2025

Welcome to the SCR Animisha APP! Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. By using SCR Animisha APP, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Provision of information

All viewers can access the latest Board vigilance circulars to improve knowledge and awareness. Various SCR system improvements and related circulars are made available to other zonal railways to support transparency across the South Central Railway zone.

2. Sharing of Information

Information is shared with Railway Board circulars and within the SCR zone to coordinate with zonal railway departments for improved efficiency and transparency.

3. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding data processed by the app:

  • Access or read posted circular information.
  • Delete your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for specific data usage.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: Email: sdgm@scr.railnet.gov.in

4. Retention of Information

We retain information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy or as required by applicable law.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any significant changes will be communicated through the app or via email. Please review this policy regularly to stay informed.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

Email: sdgm@scr.railnet.gov.in

Phone: 9701370091

7. Consent

By downloading and using the SCR Animisha APP, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of information as described in this Privacy Policy.
